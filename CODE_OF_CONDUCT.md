# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful space for users and contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent and communicate clearly
- focus feedback on code, docs, and behavior rather than people
- help keep discussions accessible to newcomers

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- trolling, deliberate intimidation, or sustained disruption
- publishing private information without permission
- abusive or exclusionary language

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub before posting public details.
